## Selected Design Approach: **Playful Kawaii Minimalism**

I've chosen the **Playful Kawaii Minimalism** approach for this Valentine's Day application. This design philosophy perfectly captures the cute, romantic, and intimate nature of your gift to Melissa while maintaining excellent usability.

### Why This Approach:
- **Jin Miran stickers** become the emotional centerpiece, reacting to her answers and celebrating her correct responses
- **Soft pastel palette** (rose, mint, lavender) creates a warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for a romantic quiz
- **Generous whitespace** keeps the focus on the personal questions and memories you're sharing
- **Playful animations** make the experience feel interactive and fun, not just informational
- **Asymmetric layout** with floating stickers creates visual interest without feeling cluttered

### Key Design Decisions:
1. **Color System**: Rose (#FFB6C1), Mint (#B0E0E6), Lavender (#E6D5F0) on warm off-white background
2. **Typography**: Quicksand (titles) + Poppins (body) for a friendly, modern feel
3. **Sticker Integration**: Jin Miran characters float around the page, react to answers, and celebrate correct responses
4. **Animations**: Gentle floating motions, bounce effects on correct answers, physics-based button evasion
5. **Layout**: Card-based with diagonal dividers, asymmetric positioning for visual interest

This approach balances cuteness with sophistication, making the experience feel personal and special.
